Luggage Please

At Rubens Games I programmed and integrated several mechanics for "Luggage Please" (Unity). Below are the three most important ones I created. Art assets were provided by the studio.

Advanced Suitcase & Item Physics System

  • Dynamic item physics (activates when suitcase is open).
  • Free item manipulation (grabbing, rotating, inserting/removing).
  • Features for resetting item position and a visual drop indicator.
  • Anti-closure mechanism to prevent closing if obstructed.

X-Ray Scanner with Material Differentiation

  • Scans suitcase contents and displays on screen.
  • Color-coded distinction for metal, organic matter, and other materials.

Contextual Interaction Wheel

  • Intuitive menu for NPC/environment interaction.
  • Dynamic options based on location (e.g. different actions at X-ray vs suitcase scale).
